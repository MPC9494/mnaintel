Data Sources & Methodology
  1. Public EV/EBITDA multiples — Damodaran, NYU Stern, January 2026. Positive-EBITDA firms, trailing 12-month data.
  2. Private company discount — DealStats Value Index Q1 2025; Bain & Co M&A Report 2025; FE International 2025. Micro (<€500k EBITDA): −55–65%; Small (€500k–€2M): −35–50%; Mid (€2M–€5M): −20–35%.
  3. Buyer type adjustments — CLFI M&A EV/EBITDA report Q2 2025. PE median 10.1× vs Corporate 8.6× (US); EU: PE 11.2× vs Corporate 8.5×.
  4. Size-based multiple scaling — DealFlowAgent 2025; Comparables.ai 2025 guide. Micro businesses: 20–40% of public multiples; small: 40–70%.
