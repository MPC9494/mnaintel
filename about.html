What M&A Intelligence is

A weekly briefing written from the buy side, for business owners thinking about selling — whether in one year or five. Each issue covers one concept in depth: how buyers price a business, what kills deals in due diligence, how earn-outs are structured, what management dependency does to a multiple.

The content comes from real transaction experience — not textbooks. What I actually see when I open a data room, sit across from a seller, or build an acquisition model.

Free to subscribe. No advisory service. No broker mandate. The only agenda is closing the information gap between buyers and sellers.
