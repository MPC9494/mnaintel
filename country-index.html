Data sources
  1. Advisory fees — Firmex/DealCircle European M&A Fee Guide 2024–2025. 289 European M&A advisors surveyed. Average success fee: 5.5% on €5M deals, declining to ~2% on €100M+.
  2. Legal & Due Diligence — KPMG, Deloitte, PwC M&A cost benchmarks. Varies by deal complexity. Cross-border deals add 25–40% to legal costs.
  3. Capital gains tax — National tax authority data and EY/KPMG country tax guides 2025. Rates shown for individual shareholders; corporate rates differ significantly.
  4. Stamp duty / transfer tax — EU Commission and national data. Share deals typically exempt in most jurisdictions; asset deals attract real estate transfer taxes where applicable.
  5. Total cost estimate — excludes buyer-side advisory, financing costs, and post-merger integration. Sell-side perspective only.
